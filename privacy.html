Privacy Policy for History Eraser Pro

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

1. Data Collection

History Eraser Pro does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data, browsing history, or user information. Everything happens locally on your device.

2. Permissions Justification

The extension requires the following permissions strictly for its core functionality:

  • history: Required to detect and delete browsing history entries in real-time.
  • browsingData: Required to perform scheduled and manual batch cleanups of browsing records.
  • alarms: Required to run background scheduled cleanups.

3. Data Usage

The data accessed by this extension is used exclusively for the purpose of deleting it. We do not sell or transfer your data to any third parties.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support page.
